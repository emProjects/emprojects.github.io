TMAC

Basement

Tech Lab

From having computer problems to wanting to build a cool robot, the tech lab is the place to go. The tech lab is home to EPS's maker space, which is equipped with 3D printers, laser cutters, arduino components and much more. For many years, this space also oversaw Fusor, a project hoping to achieve nuclear fusion in TMAC's own basement. Clubs, including robotics, regularly meet in this space.

Amphitheater

Upon entering TMAC, you are immediately greeted by the amphitheater. This space is used for class meetings, guest speakers, and general assemblies. At times, the stage of the amphitheater is used to set up arenas for robotics competitions. Additionally, before TALI was built, the amphitheater was used as the school's theater.

First Floor

Chem Room

The first floor of TMAC only has a couple classrooms, but the chemistry lab is an important lab. The chemistry lab contains an isolated experiment chamber, six lab stations, and a whole lot of dangerous chemicals. If you ever take chemistry, advanced chemistry, or organic chemistry, you will find yourself in this room.

Second Floor

Biology Lab

The second floor of TALI is home to one of two biology labs on the EPS campus. The biology lab is equipped with tools needed to conduct many experimental setups, ranging from calorimetry to PCR. Similarly to the chem lab, there are also six lab stations. You are lickely to have a class in this room if you take biology or advanced biology.

Third and Fourth Floor

Gymnasium

The gymansium is one of the most verstalile spaces on the EPS campus. Using different markings on the floor and a combination of nets, hoops, and balls, the space can be transformed between courts for basketball, volleyball, handball, and much more. You will likely spend time in the gym, either when completing PE credits or if you participate in an extracurricular sport.

Mezzanine

Located in a balcony connected to the gymnasium, the mezzanine is equipped with anything you may need for personalized training. Pull up bars, weight lifting machines, treadmills, and work-out equipment line the walls. It is worth noting that the mezzanine is only accessible during clubs time, PE classes, extracurricular sports, or by special request.
