The sport court is located behind TALI and TMAC when you are looking at the buildings from the entrance to the school. It is where drop off and pick up happen and is a hub for community bonding and fun during lunch, middle bands, and free periods.

The outside parking spaces are located at the entrance to the school from Northeast 38th Place. They are great for senior and sometimes junior parking. There are also spaces for guests to the school, guests of the head of school, and more. It is also the location of the school and sports buses.

There are tables located in between TALI, TMAC, and the LPC where students can do work, eat lunch, and hang out.
