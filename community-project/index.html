Why we created this?

The most scary part about going to a new school is trying to find your way to classes. Not only that, much time are wasted trying to explore the campus and figuring out by youself where the shortcuts are and where some "special" spaces are. This website is to help you to save the time trying to figure out the campus by yourself, and to deepen your understanding of the EPS campus.

TALI Building

TALI is the newest building on campus. It contains the threater, music rooms, a biology lab, classrooms, etc. It is four stories tall and has a beautiful rooftop area.

TMAC Building

TMAC is the second newest building. It contains the Upper School makerspace, tech office, gym, and other classrooms. It is also four stories tall.

LPC Building

The LPC is the oldest of the three building mentioned. It is only two stories tall. The LPC contains the kitchen, cafeteria, library, etc.

Outside Areas

There is a sports court at the back of the school. Different type of activites, such as basketball, ultimate frisbee, tetherball, can be done. There are also many plants around the outside area.
