Color Scheme of the Week


About this Color Scheme

For this assignment, I used a complementary color scheme. The primary color is blue, with five different shades being drawn from the sky. The secondary color is white, used to balance out the saturated shades of blue and make the dark text easy to read. The tertiary color is yellow, drawn from the seagull's beak. I want this website to feel playful and calm, like the user is looking a simplified color-scheme of a beach.
